The Rules

TLDR; it's a scavenger hunt. Your team will get clues via SMS or on this site. You have to check-in at the solution to get the next clue. Email your answers for bonus questions to companytrip@foursquare.com. Have fun, but don't miss the bus back!

This Scavenger Hunt will cover historic and modern points of interest in Old San Juan!

Things to pack for the hunt: hat, sun block, water, comfortable walking shoes, phone/camera.

Upon arrival in Old Town, we'll be at Plaza Colon. Check into the plaza on Swarm using your personal Foursquare account -- AFTER you have logged in to this site and updated your phone number -- and you'll begin receiving your team's clues via SMS! Check into venues as you discover them to uncover your next clues. You can also see a list of all the clues you've uncovered on this site at any time in case you don't get the text or forget what the clue was.

We'll be in Old Town for 2.5 hours. Use as little or as much time to complete the hunt as your team would like, timing does not affect scoring. But, do not miss the 5:30pm bus or your team will be disqualified and you'll need to find your own transportation back to the hotel.

Answers are 1 point. Bonus Answers are 2 points.

Answers are submitted by someone on your team checking in to the solution.

All bonus answers should be submitted via email to companytrip@foursquare.com in picture or video form and captioned with your team name. If a question requests you write an answer, you can simply jot it down in your phone "notes" and send a screen shot. Paper/pens will not be necessary.

Only answers submitted before 5:30pm will count.
